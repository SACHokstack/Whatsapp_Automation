# Company

Timmins Training & Consulting Sdn. Bhd. is a Malaysia-based corporate training and consulting company specialising in engineering, software, technology, AI, data analytics, and professional development programs.

We deliver both public and in-house training programs for organisations across Malaysia and the Asia-Pacific region.

Industries we serve:
- Semiconductor
- Electronics Manufacturing
- Software Development
- Banking & Financial Services
- Telecommunications
- Government Agencies
- Shared Services
- Higher Education

Some of our clients include Intel, Altera, Bosch, Motorola Solutions, Qualcomm, Cisco, Citibank, DBS Bank, Hong Leong Bank, Maxis, U Mobile, and Infineon.

Timmins delivers HRDC-registered training programs and can provide HRDC registration details for eligible courses upon request.

Timmins can also support vendor registration and procurement onboarding activities.
