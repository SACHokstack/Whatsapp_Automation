# Certification

Participants receive:
- Printed certificate of completion
- Electronic certificate of completion

The certificate is issued after successful participation in the training program.

The training fee includes:
- Training delivery
- PDF training materials
- Morning refreshments
- Lunch
- Afternoon refreshments
- Printed certificate
- Electronic certificate
- Hands-on exercises and labs

---
If the lead asks what certification they will get, mention both printed and electronic completion certificates.
